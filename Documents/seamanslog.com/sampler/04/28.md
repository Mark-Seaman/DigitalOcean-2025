# To DO and To BE

I often define myself by what I DO

    My career
    My accomplishments
    My goals
    My contributions
    My influence
    My position
    My money
    My productivity

I seldom define myself by who I AM

    My character
    My peaceful mind
    My capacity to love
    My relationships
    My resiliency
    My understanding
    My growth
